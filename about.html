Our Statement

The Bathory Bloodmobile and Bathory Blood Donation business is an accessible location for residents of Bellingham Washington to donate blood. Donors can either make individual appointments or sign up to host blood drives and invite their community to come donate. The Bathory Bloodmobile is operated by a team of certified nurses who take great care of every donor. Donations made through Bathory Blood go straight back to local people; Bathory Blood believes in supporting the community and works to support underprivileged communities who may not otherwise be able to easily access blood when necessary. This may include both the financially challenged as well as the hungry creatures of the night.
